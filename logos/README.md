# University Logos Directory

Place your university logos here:

## Required Files:
- `northeastern-logo.png` - Northeastern University logo
- `giki-logo.png` - Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute logo

## Recommended Specifications:
- **Format**: PNG (with transparency preferred)
- **Size**: 240x160px maximum (logos will be auto-scaled)
- **Background**: Transparent
- **Quality**: High resolution but optimized

## Where to Get Logos:
- University official websites
- Brand guidelines pages
- Vector formats converted to PNG

## Notes:
- Logos will be displayed in black and white (filtered) to match the dark theme
- They'll appear at the top of each education card
- Make sure logos are clear and recognizable at smaller sizes
